How to Play

To start the game, begin by tapping on the "Start"button.

Each player can use upto 4 rolls to fill an empty box on their side.

Each player must meet or beat the basic rule for each category or take a 0 for that box.

Choose a category that works best with your current dice.

Next, tap on any dice you want to save then push the "Roll the Dice" button to spin the remaining dice.

When you are satisfied with your roll, tap the empty box next to the category you have chosen. This will display the number of points you can receive.

Tap the "Save Roll" button to save your score and end your turn.

How to win

HOW TO WIN THE GAME !!


When all the boxes are filled, the game will total up all your points.


This will include the top half points, the bottom half points and any bonuses you might have earned.


The player with the most points WINS


And remember, the faster you play the less boxes are scrambled shut and the more points can be earned.

Scramble timer

The 60 second timer counts down to zero.


When it reaches zero one of the Categories


is randomly shut for both player.


Every 60 seconds the timer starts again.


So play as fast as you can to try

and keep as many of the high scoring boxes as possible!
