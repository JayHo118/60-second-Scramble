Welcome 60 Second Scarmble!


Player 1 click on Roll the Dice  to start the game.



You have 4 rolls per turn to create your best score.

Click on the dice you want to hold.

Click Roll the Dice to spin the remaining dice.

When you are satisfied with your Roll, select a box you where want to save your score in and

Click on Save Roll button to end your turn.

But hurry because in 60 seconds 1 of the categories are going to be scrambled shut

To regain the closed category you have to throw a High Five

The scramble category changes category every 15 seconds

Have fun and enjoy the game !!
